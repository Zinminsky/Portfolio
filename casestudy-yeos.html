Project Case Study - Development Journey

Overview Background
Role Web Designer and Front End Developer
Timeline 3 Months
Tools Photoshop, Visual Studio Code, WordPress
Development HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Jason, Jquery
Company Website YEO'S

Initial Stage

In the initial stage, I created several design concepts based on the project goals and user needs. These designs were presented to the client to gather feedback on layout, visuals, and overall usability. Based on the client's input, I refined and iterated on the designs, making necessary adjustments until the final design was approved.

Middle Stage

During the middle stage, I closely collaborated with the development team to ensure the approved designs were properly implemented within the WordPress CMS. This involved aligning design components with technical constraints, adjusting layouts for responsiveness, and ensuring design consistency across templates while maintaining usability and performance.

Final Stage

In the final stage, I reviewed the entire website to ensure all content, visuals, and interactions were accurate and consistent with the approved designs. After conducting final checks and quality assurance, the website was successfully launched, delivering a polished and user-ready product.
