NUS Art Festival

NUS Arts Festival (NAF) celebrates its 7th year with exciting explorations of the theme of Asian Cities. This enables cities in Asia to define today’s modern city in very different ways from their Western counterparts. Over 3 weekends in March 2012 at the University Cultural Centre (UCC), we pay tribute to the Asian spirit in music, dance, theatre and film.
